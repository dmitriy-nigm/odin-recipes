Risotto

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • ⅔ cup sliced green onion
  • 1 ⅓ cups uncooked long-grain rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir green onion in hot butter briefly, then add rice.
  2. Cook and stir until rice is toasted, 2 to 3 minutes. Pour in water; season with chicken bouillon and pepper.
  3. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to medium-low. Cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat. Let stand, covered, for 5 minutes. Stir in Parmesan cheese.
